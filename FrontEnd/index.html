About This Website

Welcome to CryptoLab — an educational platform for students to explore and test various cryptographic algorithms. This website allows you to perform both encryption and decryption operations for different classical and modern cryptography techniques.

Classical algorithms such as Caesar Cipher, Affine Cipher, Playfair Cipher, Hill Cipher, Rail Fence Cipher, Columnar Transposition Cipher, and Vigenère Cipher demonstrate the foundations of cryptography.

Modern algorithms such as SDES, DES, and AES represent advanced encryption standards used in real-world data security systems.
